BLOCKCHAIN BASED ASSET MANAGEMENT

This product registers all the information about a vehicle during its lifecycle, to increase the transparency of the vehicle condition.

This transparency brings value to the second-hand cars market. The information can also be put to other uses, e.g. negotiate better insurance premiums.

All the Vehicles are registered with its VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) and public address in a blockchain.

With the control of the vehicle owner, third parties will be able to query the blockchain for information about the vehicle, and append events like:

  • - Maintenance - changed parts, damaged parts, number of kilometers, etc.)
  • - Insurance – badge for no accidents on that year
  • - Selling of the car
